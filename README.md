📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI. The dashboard helps HR teams monitor workforce trends, employee demographics, salary insights, and performance metrics to support better business decisions.

🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze employee data and answer key HR questions such as:

What is the total workforce?
Which age group has the highest number of employees?
What is the average salary hike?
How does performance rating vary with salary hike?
What is the gender distribution?
Which department has the highest employee count?
What are the experience levels across employees?
🛠️ Tools Used

🔍 Key Insights
Employees aged 26–35 represent the largest portion of the workforce.
The Research & Development department has the highest number of employees.
Salary hikes are distributed across both male and female employees with slight variation.
Employees with more years at the company generally contribute significantly to overall experience.
Performance ratings vary across salary hike percentages, helping identify compensation trends.
